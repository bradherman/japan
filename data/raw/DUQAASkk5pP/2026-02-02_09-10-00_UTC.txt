Things I wish I knew before coming to Japan 🇯🇵

Japan is incredibly safe, clean, and convenient—but there are a few things that surprised me.

• Cash is still king. Many small shops don’t accept credit cards.
• Trash cans are rare, so you’ll often carry your trash with you.
• Trains are extremely punctual—being even one minute late matters.
• Speaking Japanese isn’t required, but learning a few phrases goes a long way.
• Convenience stores are on another level. Food, drinks, even full meals—24/7.

Once you know these, traveling in Japan becomes even more enjoyable.
