I did a lot of research on skincare and fashion items to buy in Japan, so feel free to steal this list 😃

Pro Tips:
* Buy Onitsuka Tiger shoes from Tokyo as there are multiple outlet options compared to Kyoto or Osaka
* Don’t forget to buy Omamori which are Japanese good luck charms for prosperity, good health, success in studies and more. These are usually sold near temples
* Souvenir shopping is best around Sensoji Temple, Asakusa. Here you’ll find plenty of options at more affordable prices
* Always carry your passport for tax-free shopping
* Shop from Uniqlo for high quality clothing at reasonable prices. They also have great bag options
