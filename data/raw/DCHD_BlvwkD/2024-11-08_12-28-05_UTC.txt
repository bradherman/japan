For the ultimate coffee experience, you need this spot on your Tokyo itinerary. Heck it’s worth booking a Japan trip just to come here!

📌 Koffee Mameya Kakeru 
(their Kiyosumi Shirakawa location, not the one in Shibuya as they have different concepts) 

You can choose one of their 3 omakase menus. Or go a la carte (their espresso martini and cheesecake are supposed to be *amazing*)

💰 Prices start at ¥6,500 and go up to ¥10,000 ($42-65) for the tasting menu so it’s not a cheap experience for our fellow mid-budget travellers. But, as coffee lovers, we thought it was worth every penny

The baristas seriously know what they’re about and their coffee/food pairing were sensational 

📢 Just make sure to book in advance as they don’t have many seats
