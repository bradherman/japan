A fairy-tale cave bar near Tokyo ✨🕯️🍹

This spot just turned one and came highly recommended by my local friend. The cave-like interiors, warm glow and curved walls make the whole space feel super cozy. The cocktails are beautiful and creative, with subtle colors, a little shimmer, and really balanced flavors! Met Futa, Kaede and Hirose behind the bar—all super fun, nice, and easy to chat with. Such a great spot to end the night!!
📍 Bar Now (Kawasaki)
