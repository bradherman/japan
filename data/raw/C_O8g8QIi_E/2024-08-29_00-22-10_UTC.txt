A life-changing Michelin 3-star cheesecake 🧀

ミシュラン三つ星屋のチーズケーキ

Chef @kishidashuzo of Tokyo’s famed Michelin 3-star French restaurant Quintessence first developed this one-of-a-kind cheesecake as a way to keep his staff and restaurant afloat during the pandemic. Unwilling to settle for anything less than perfect, Chef Kishida spent years perfecting his recipe.

This three-layer cheesecake starts with a tart base full of dried nuts, followed by a middle layer of rare cheesecake blended with cream cheese and sour cream, and finally a top layer of baked cheesecake made with rare 36-month-aged Comté cheese.

Each layer creates a specific balance and harmony between sweetness and acidity, texture and mouthfeel, and form and function.

A single bite will challenge everything you know about cheesecakes, as you experience the vast difference that 17 consecutive years of Michelin 3-star excellence makes.

The cake is available for delivery only and can be ordered via @omakasejapan.
