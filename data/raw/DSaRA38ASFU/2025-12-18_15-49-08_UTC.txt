Tokyo’s lucky cat–themed vinyl jazz bar 🐱🎶

Hidden upstairs in Shinjuku, this spot has been run by a husband-and-wife team since 1979. Inside, there are 2,500+ maneki-neko (招財貓) everywhere—lining the walls, stacked in cabinets, basically watching you drink.

It’s all vinyl jazz, picked by the owner Miyazaki-san, with signed album sleeves on the walls from his time in New York back in the ’70s. Super cozy, a little quirky, and very Tokyo.

Snacks are around ¥350–¥850 (~$2–6 USD) and drinks are around ¥700 ($5 USD).
