📍Koffee Mameya Kakeru, Tokyo | #koffeemameyakakeru

We left @koffee_mameya_kakeru feeling like we just watched a spectacular concert 🤩

Koffee Mameya Kakeru leans completely into its philosophy of ‘kakeru’ which means ‘to multiply’ or the concept of various elements coming together to create something more significant and valuable than each individual part.

Focusing on the Japanese concept of ‘kodawari’ - aspiration to perfection and attention to detail, the master brewers at Koffee Mameya Kakeru focus on stretching coffee’s potential and delivering the best cup possible. 

Tasting courses start at ¥3500, which comes with 3 drinks brewed using different methods, accompanied by complementing sweets. Premium coffee course and cocktail coffee course are available too! 

Note: The banana au-lait in the video was an a-la-carte order

Reservations are a MUST and is available on their page
