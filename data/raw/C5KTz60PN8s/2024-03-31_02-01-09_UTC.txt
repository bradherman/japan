Things that exist only in Japan.

Choco Monaka Jumbo is one of my favorite ice creams of all time! It’s so popular that one bar gets sold every 0.17 seconds. 

The ice cream is covered in a crispy monaka and it has delicious piece of chocolate inside

You can find it in almost every convenience store. 

But before you it be sure to put in the toaster for 30 seconds. This will make your choco monaka jumbo taste even better!
