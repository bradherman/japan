Have you ever seen a wagyu fat-fried cheeseburger? 🍔

元祖の切ったやつは東京に来た！

@briskstand_asakusa is the first Tokyo location of the famous Kobe burger joint known for their original way of cooking cheeseburgers. 100% domestic Japanese beef is topped with caramelized onions and cheese before being cut in half, and then cooked again in its own fat, creating a crispy, burnt inside packed with all kinds of umami.
