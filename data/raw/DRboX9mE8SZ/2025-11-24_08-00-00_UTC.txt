Tokyo Food Top 5!
Number 5: BAR CENTIFOLIA.
A refined performance crafted by Japan’s top bartender. A must-visit place at least once in your life.
Number 4: Shunya-chan.
A rare street-style ramen shop in Tokyo! The clear broth is so delicious you can drink it all up!
Number 3: Marushichi.
The thickest katsudon in Japan! Full of volume, with tender, juicy meat that’s perfect for a hearty meal!
Number 2: Benitsuru.
A long-loved spot for its iconic triple-layered bacon egg pancakes! A must-try, but reservations are essential.
Number 1: Ikura.
Enjoy the live cooking experience of omelette rice on a hot plate! The perfect pairing of omelette and hamburger steak is amazing!
Make sure to check them out!
