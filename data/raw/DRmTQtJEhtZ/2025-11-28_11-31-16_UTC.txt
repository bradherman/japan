Considered the wagyu of chicken, Jidori is the most rare and luxurious type of chicken to try in Japan! 

🏆It’s so prestigious that only 1% of the chicken in Japan meets the high requirements to be considered Jidori, including;
✅ Sustainability - raised slowly with a strict standard of care and feeding 
✅ Quality - superior taste and texture (rich with a firm bite)
✅ Local varieties - raised in specific regions across Japan, each brand of Jidori has its own unique flavor, texture, and cultural background that can’t be replicated or compared with mass-produced chicken 

📍Only specialty restaurants serve Jidori chicken, including MARU in Tokyo! They use a special breed of Jidori called Amakusa Daoi in several dishes such as their incredible Premium Jidori bowl topped with grilled Jidori, truffle, caviar, and foie gras. 
 
🐓Don’t leave Japan without trying this rare delicacy!
