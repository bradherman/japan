Part 1 of Must-Buy Items in Japan 🇯🇵💕
 Save this for your next Japan trip!
Shopping in Japan can feel overwhelming but in the best way possible. It’s truly a paradise for snacks and beauty finds, and with limited luggage space, choosing what to buy isn’t easy 🥲
In this video, I’m sharing my top must-buy recommendations from Japan that are totally worth it.

Pro tips: • Check out Don Quijote, but don’t skip gems like Daiso or Seria • Always carry your passport for tax-free shopping • Leave extra space in your luggage (or pack an extra bag for shopping) • Pick up convenience store merch like 7-Eleven socks and Lawson tote bags • You can’t go wrong with Canmake beauty products as they are amazing across all categories
