Sharing this affordable omakase spot in Tokyo located right at Tsukiji 🥹🍣

📍Sushi Yamaharu Tsukiji 山治 築地 鮨
🏹From the main street , enter this small alley , find the small wooden signage and climb the stairs , push open the door and walk all the way to 3rd floor and you will find this amazing Omakase place.

🔗 This place only accept reservation which can be made online with Tablecheck / Tabelog.
🔗 https://www.tablecheck.com/en/sushi-yamaharu/reserve/message

💳💰Accept both Cash and Card.
5500¥ / per person / 15 course

Sushi Lover ! Don't miss out this place if you are visiting Tokyo !🇯🇵👀
