I’ve been to listening bars all over the world, and so many of them trace their inspiration back to Japan. Sitting inside of Grandfather’s for a while, it’s hard not to feel like this is one of the most authentic I’ve ever experienced. Five decades of history, dim lights, cigarette smoke, and music humming beneath the streets of Tokyo. For the first time, it feels like I’ve reached the source.

There’s still so much more of Tokyo’s music scene to experience so make sure to follow Black Vinyl Stories and we’ll keep exploring.
