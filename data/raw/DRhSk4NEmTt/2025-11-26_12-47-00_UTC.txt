You will regret skipping this place if you’re looking for cool, made-in-Japan souvenirs.

We’re here at BEAMS JAPAN, located in Shinjuku.
This six floors store brings together everything made in Japan. From clothing and crafts to art and so much more.

From sushi-themed cups to Mount Fuji–inspired ceramics, from traditional Japanese cushions to unique daruma dolls, you’ll find tons of cute and well-designed items.

On-the-second floor, you’ll find Japanese pop-culture goods—everything from comically designed T-shirts to items inspired by classic video games. Some pieces are created by local artists, making them things you won’t easily find anywhere else.

You can also find practical items, like mini keychain purses and wristwatches with kanji on the dial instead of regular numbers.

On the other floors, you’ll find home and lifestyle items, such as small interior goods and kitchen-related products. They also carry traditional craft pieces like sake cups and seasonal decorations.

Their main store is actually a clothing select shop, and of course you can buy clothes here as well. If you’re interested in Japanese fashion, definitely check it out too!
