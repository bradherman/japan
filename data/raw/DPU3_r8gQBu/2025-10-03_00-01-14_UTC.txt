If you’re already shopped at Donki or the Beams store and want another spot for cool gifts, don’t forget to check out LOFT!

If Donki is the Walmart of Japan, LOFT is more like the Target of Japan. Cleaner aesthetic, better merchandising, and higher quality products. 

This six floor building has a great selection of stationery, home goods, beauty/skincare, watches, and travel bags so come here if you’re looking for those. The quality is a step up, and you’ll products at all price points!
