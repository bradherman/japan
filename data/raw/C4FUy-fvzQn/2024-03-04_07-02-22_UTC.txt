CARBONARA UDON in Tokyo!

You’ve probably heard of the famous Shin Udon in Shinjuku that is known for their carbonara udon, but we don’t really like waiting in lines 😅

So we tried out this spot instead and we have to say, it was pretty good!

It’s the back streets of Harajuku and it’s got such an edgy vibe like all the local streetwear shops around 🙌🏼

When we arrived at around 5pm there was no line, so we didn’t have to wait at all!

The carbonara udon is 950 yen and comes with butter, parmesan bacon and raw egg so you’ve gotta mix it all together to emulsify - be warned, if you hate the thought of eating raw egg then this probably isn’t the place for you 😝

Overall would totally recommend!
