# Ritz-Carlton Tokyo — Teppanyaki Recommendations

Source: Ritz-Carlton Tokyo concierge guide
Location context: distances are relative to Ritz-Carlton Tokyo (Roppongi/Midtown area)

### Hinokizaka Teppan
- **Area:** 45th floor, Ritz-Carlton Tokyo
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 8,000, Dinner ~JPY 30,000
- **Notes:** In-hotel teppanyaki. Chef presents selection of fine cuts from Japan's best cattle growers for you to choose, then grills to perfection.
- **Link:** https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/japan/tokyo/dining/hinokizaka#fndtn-Teppanyaki

### Kisentei Tokyo Midtown
- **Area:** Tokyo Midtown (~5 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 10,000, Dinner ~JPY 20,000
- **Notes:** Uses Ningyocho Imahan Kuroge Wagyu beef. Private rooms available with counter seating.
- **Link:** https://gurunavi.com/en/g073908/rst/
- **Phone:** 03-5413-7988

### Steak Hama
- **Area:** Roppongi (~5 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 8,000, Dinner ~JPY 30,000
- **Notes:** Founded 1964 — classic flavor loved for generations. A la carte and course menus. Offers meat, vegetables, and seafood.
- **Link:** https://gurunavi.com/en/g063706/rst/
- **Phone:** 03-3403-1717

### Bifteck Kawamura Roppongi
- **Area:** Roppongi (~5 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Weekdays: Dinner only. Weekends: Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Tuesdays
- **Budget:** Dinner ~JPY 50,000
- **Notes:** Endless pursuit of Kobe Beef excellence. A la carte and course menus. Their Hamburg (hamburger patty) is a must-order.
- **Link:** https://www.bifteck.co.jp/en/restaurant/roppongi/
- **Phone:** 03-5775-6633

### Seryna Mon Cher Ton Ton
- **Area:** Roppongi (~5 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Weekdays: Dinner only. Weekends: Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 10,000, Dinner ~JPY 20,000
- **Notes:** Uses only finest beef strain. A la carte and course menus. Also has Shinjuku location. Gluten free available.
- **Link:** http://www.seryna.co.jp/en/monchertonton/roppongi/
- **Phone:** 03-3402-1055

### Morimoto XEX
- **Area:** Roppongi (~5 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Dinner only
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** ~JPY 25,000
- **Notes:** Modern meets traditional. A la carte menu. Gluten free available. Also has an authentic sushi counter with omakase and a la carte sushi.
- **Link:** http://www.xexgroup.jp/en/morimoto
- **Phone:** 03-3479-0065

### Ukaitei Omotesando
- **Area:** Omotesando (~15 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Wednesdays & New Year season
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 15,000, Dinner ~JPY 30,000
- **Notes:** All private rooms with hot griddles — dedicated chef cooks in front of you. Modern atmosphere with art. Also has branches in Ginza and Roppongi.
- **Link:** http://www.ukai.co.jp/omotesando/
- **Phone:** 03-5467-5252
