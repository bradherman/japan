# Ritz-Carlton Tokyo — Casual Sushi Recommendations

Source: Ritz-Carlton Tokyo concierge guide
Location context: distances are relative to Ritz-Carlton Tokyo (Roppongi/Midtown area)

### Sushi Zanmai Takumi (Roppongi RAPIROSU store)
- **Area:** Roppongi, Tokyo (~8 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner (open 24 hours)
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** ~JPY 6,000+
- **Notes:** One of Japan's most popular casual sushi chains. Fun, local, casual a la carte dining.
- **Link:** http://www.kiyomura.co.jp/
- **Phone:** 03-5772-1005

### Iki na Sushi Dokoro Abe
- **Area:** Roppongi, Tokyo (~15 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 3,000+, Dinner ~JPY 10,000+
- **Menu:** Course or a la carte
- **Notes:** Authentic Edo-mae ambience at reasonable prices.
- **Link:** https://ikinasushidokoroabe.jp/kodawari.html (Japanese only)
- **Phone:** 03-3589-1818

### Kinka Sushi Bar Izakaya Roppongi
- **Area:** Roppongi, Tokyo (~7 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 3,000+, Dinner ~JPY 8,000+
- **Notes:** Reverse-imported from Canada. Classic nigiri plus creative items: Canadian lobster sushi/sashimi, blowtorched pressed sushi, gorgeous sashimi platters.
- **Link:** http://kinkasushibarizakaya.com/jp/
- **Phone:** 050-5596-1874

### Sushi Gonpachi Nishiazabu
- **Area:** Nishi-Azabu, Tokyo (~15 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Dinner only (until 2 AM)
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** ~JPY 6,000+
- **Notes:** 3rd floor of Gonpachi Nishi-Azabu (casual izakaya). Vegan menu available. Late night option.
- **Link:** https://gonpachi.jp/sushi-nishi-azabu/
- **Phone:** 03-5771-0180

### Midori Sushi Akasaka
- **Area:** Akasaka, Tokyo (~10 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** January 1st
- **Budget:** Lunch ~JPY 2,000, Dinner ~JPY 5,000–6,000
- **Notes:** Amazing quality at very reasonable prices. Experienced sushi chefs. Kids friendly.
- **Link:** https://www.sushinomidori.co.jp/shops/akasaka/
- **Phone:** 03-5545-5516
