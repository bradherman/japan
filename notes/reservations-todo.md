## NOTES & TIPS

**Golden Week Impact (Apr 29 - May 6):**
- Expect 2-3x normal crowds at major attractions
- Domestic tourism peaks - Japanese families traveling
- Book everything possible in advance for this period
- Trains and hotels fill up months ahead (yours are already booked - good!)

**Reservation Strategy:**
- Japanese restaurants often release tables at specific times (midnight, noon, 20th of month)
- Set phone alarms/calendar reminders for exact booking times
- Have credit card and details ready before booking window opens
- Some restaurants prefer phone calls over online (especially Michelin spots)

**Time Zone:**
- Japan is UTC+9 (no daylight saving)
- California is UTC-7 (PDT in April-May)
- Japan is 16 hours ahead of Pacific Time
- Midnight JST = 8:00 AM previous day PDT

**Backup Plans:**
- If Ghibli Museum sells out: Look for Ghibli Store/themed cafes in Tokyo
- If Pizza Bar 38th fails: RistoPizza or other high-end pizza spots
- If Michelin sushi fully booked: Try other excellent sushi spots from Ritz-Carlton list (Taku, Masashi, Kanesaka)
