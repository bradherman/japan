# Transport Day-by-Day Cheatsheet

**Trip: April 25 - May 10, 2026**
**Route: Tokyo → Kyoto → Osaka → Hakone → Tokyo → Narita**

> Suica/IC card covers all local trains/subways/buses. Shinkansen requires separate tickets (SmartEX app).
> Google Maps works perfectly for real-time transit directions in Japan.

- [ ] Set up **Apple Wallet Suica** before landing (Wallet → + → Transit Card → Suica → Load ¥5,000)
- [ ] Download **SmartEX app** and register credit card (for Shinkansen booking)

## Luggage Forwarding (Takkyubin) Schedule

*You said you'll have 1 carry-on + 1 backpack per person. With only a carry-on, you CAN take luggage on trains. Takkyubin is still nice for convenience but may not be necessary with light packing.*

| Send Date (morning) | From → To | Arrives | Cost/suitcase |
|---------------------|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Apr 29 | Shibuya Granbell → Hotel Forza Kyoto | Apr 30 | ~¥2,400-3,100 |
| May 3 | Hotel Forza Kyoto → Cross Hotel Osaka | May 4 | ~¥1,800-2,200 |
| May 6 | Cross Hotel Osaka → Hakone Gora Byakudan | May 7 | ~¥2,400-2,800 |
| May 8 | Hakone Gora Byakudan → Hotel MyStays Nippori | May 9 | ~¥2,200-2,600 |

**With carry-on only:** You probably don't need takkyubin. A carry-on fits on the Shinkansen overhead rack easily. The only tricky leg is Hakone Tozan Railway (narrow mountain train) — but still manageable with a carry-on. **Recommend skipping takkyubin and carrying your bags** to save ~¥9,000-11,000 total.
