# Packing List — Brad Only

**Trip:** 15 nights (Apr 25 - May 10, 2026) | Tokyo → Kyoto → Osaka → Hakone → Tokyo
**Bag strategy:** 1 carry-on roller + 1 mid-size backpack (laptop + daily carry). 1-2 collapsible duffels packed flat for purchases to check on the way home.
**Weather:** 10-25°C (50-77°F). Cool mornings/evenings, warm afternoons. Rain likely 2-4 days. Humid.
**Laundry plan:** Do laundry every 4-5 days. All your hotels have coin laundry or are near a coin laundromat (see notes below).

---

## Clothes — Tops (6)

- [ ] 1 nice short-sleeve button-down (linen or linen-blend — breathable, packs small, looks sharp for teppanyaki/omakase/birthday dinner)
- [ ] 3 lightweight t-shirts or henleys (moisture-wicking preferred — you'll walk 15-25K steps/day)
- [ ] 1 long-sleeve lightweight layer (merino or similar — doubles as evening layer)
- [ ] 1 light jacket (packable rain jacket with hood is ideal — handles both rain and cool evenings; a Patagonia Houdini or similar)

## Clothes — Bottoms (3-4)

- [ ] 2 pairs lightweight pants or chinos (one darker pair works for nice dinners)
- [ ] 1 pair athletic/jogger pants (travel days, ryokan lounging, casual walking)
- [ ] 1 pair shorts (optional — May temps can hit 25°C, useful for Hakone onsen downtime)

## Clothes — Underwear & Socks (5-6 each)

- [ ] 5-6 underwear (merino or quick-dry — can hand-wash and air dry overnight)
- [ ] 5-6 pairs socks (mix of: no-show for warm days, light crew for walking days)
- [ ] 1 pair compression socks (for the 10+ hr flights)

## Shoes (2)

- [ ] 1 pair comfortable walking shoes — SLIP-ON or easy on/off strongly recommended (you remove shoes at temples, ryokans, some restaurants, fitting rooms). Allbirds, On Cloud, or similar. Break them in before the trip.
- [ ] 1 pair sandals or slides (ryokan, hotel room, Hakone onsen area — doubles as shower shoes)

## Outerwear / Layers

- [ ] Light packable rain jacket (see above — do NOT skip this, April/May rain is real)
- [ ] Small compact umbrella (or buy a clear vinyl one at any 7-Eleven for ¥500 — Japanese convenience store umbrellas are surprisingly good)

## Backpack (Daily Carry)

- [ ] Laptop + charger
- [ ] Phone charger + cable
- [ ] Portable battery pack (10,000+ mAh — long days, lots of Google Maps)

- [ ] Reusable water bottle (tap water is safe and excellent in Japan)
- [ ] Sunglasses (see eyewear below)

## Eyewear

- [ ] Regular Rx glasses (backup — keep in backpack)

## Medicine & Health

- [ ] Tylenol / acetaminophen (headaches, general pain)
- [ ] Alka-Seltzer hangover relief (you have 4+ nights of bar-hopping planned)
- [ ] Gas-X / simethicone (new foods, rich meals, lots of fried food)
- [ ] Imodium / loperamide (just in case — better to have and not need)
- [ ] Antihistamine (Zyrtec/Claritin — spring pollen is aggressive in Japan, even if you don't have allergies at home)
- [ ] Band-aids / blister pads (Compeed or similar — critical for 20K+ step days, especially with foot surgery recovery)
- [ ] Any prescription medications (bring more than you need + keep in original bottles for customs)
- [ ] Melatonin (jet lag recovery — Japan is 16 hours ahead of PDT)

## DO NOT PACK

- **Bulky jeans** — heavy, slow to dry, take up too much bag space. Lightweight chinos do double duty.
- **More than 1 nice shirt** — you're in Japan, not a boardroom. Smart casual is the max dress code.
- **Full-size toiletries** — buy there or use Matador bottles.
- **Too many shoes** — 2 pairs max. Every extra shoe eats carry-on space you need for purchases.
- **Pocket knife / multi-tool** — can't carry in Japan (strict knife laws, even for small blades).

## Laundry Notes

**Your hotels:**

| Hotel | Dates | Laundry |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Shibuya Granbell Hotel | Apr 25-30 (5 nights) | Coin laundry in building or nearby coin laundromat. Do laundry night of Apr 28 or 29. |
| Hotel Forza Kyoto Shijo Kawaramachi | Apr 30-May 4 (4 nights) | Most Forza hotels have coin laundry rooms on a guest floor. Do laundry night of May 2. |
| Cross Hotel Osaka | May 4-7 (3 nights) | Guest coin laundry available. Do laundry night of May 5. |
| Hakone Gora Byakudan | May 7-9 (2 nights) | Ryokan has laundry service. |
| Hotel MyStays Nippori | May 9-10 (1 night) | MyStays chain always has coin laundry. |

**Coin laundry cost:** ~¥200-500 wash + ¥300-500 dry = under ¥1,000 total per load. Takes ~60-90 min. Most accept coins (stockpile ¥100 coins) and increasingly accept IC cards.

**Laundry schedule:** Wash every 4-5 days → you need 5-6 days of clothes, not 15. This is how you keep it to a carry-on.
