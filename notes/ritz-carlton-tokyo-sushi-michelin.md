# Ritz-Carlton Tokyo — Michelin Sushi Recommendations

Source: Ritz-Carlton Tokyo concierge guide
Location context: distances are relative to Ritz-Carlton Tokyo (Roppongi/Midtown area)

### Sushi SHIN (1 Michelin star)
- **Area:** Nishiazabu, Tokyo (~7 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch (except Tue & Thu) and Dinner
- **Closed:** Mondays
- **Budget:** Lunch omakase ~JPY 15,000+, Dinner ~JPY 30,000+
- **Notes:** Chef Shintaro Suzuki — 10 consecutive Michelin stars. Self-taught Edomae style, own inspiration. Attracts international clientele.
- **Link:** http://sushi-shin.com/ (Japanese only)
- **Phone:** 03-5485-0031

### Taku (1 Michelin star)
- **Area:** Nishiazabu, Tokyo (~7 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Dinner only
- **Closed:** Unknown
- **Budget:** Omakase ~JPY 30,000+
- **Notes:** Edomae tradition — nigiri and shuko served in alternating sequence. Wide menu variety.
- **Phone:** 03-5774-4372

### Sushi Masashi (1 Michelin star)
- **Area:** Kita-Aoyama, Tokyo (~10 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Sundays and holidays
- **Budget:** Omakase ~JPY 33,000
- **Notes:** Polished cuisine, hospitality cultivated in first-class hotels. Earned Michelin star within one year of opening.
- **Link:** https://www.tableall.com/restaurant/227
- **Phone:** 03-6384-5526

### Sushi Kanesaka Honten (2 Michelin stars)
- **Area:** Ginza, Tokyo (~15 min by car from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch and Dinner
- **Closed:** Unknown
- **Budget:** Omakase ~JPY 40,000+ (varies with market price)
- **Notes:** Edomae-style. Believes in the trinity of sushi: ingredients, vinegared rice, and wasabi. Serves with careful consideration of temperature, humidity, and ripeness.
- **Link:** http://www.sushi-kanesaka.com/
- **Phone:** 03-5568-4411
