# Ritz-Carlton Tokyo — Yakiniku (BBQ) Recommendations

Source: Ritz-Carlton Tokyo concierge guide
Location context: distances are relative to Ritz-Carlton Tokyo (Roppongi/Midtown area)

### Ayanokoji
- **Area:** Roppongi (~5 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Dinner only
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** ~JPY 15,000
- **Notes:** Uses finest Japanese black beef from Yoshizawa Shoten (established Ginza butcher since 1947). All private rooms.
- **Link:** http://www.ayanokouji.jp/
- **Phone:** 03-3402-1129

### Yakiniku Jinseki
- **Area:** Nishi-Azabu (~10 min drive from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Dinner only
- **Closed:** Irregular
- **Budget:** ~JPY 20,000+
- **Notes:** Premium Jinseki beef from Hiroshima Prefecture plus other top wagyu. Specialty: highly sought-after beef tongue with signature scallion and lemon sauce — must-try. Head chef has traditional Japanese cuisine background. Counter seats offer a special experience where staff grill to perfection.
- **Link:** https://yakiniku-jinseki.jp/
- **Phone:** 03-6721-1929

### Jojoen Roppongi
- **Area:** Roppongi (~7 min walk from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Lunch through dinner
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** ~JPY 10,000
- **Notes:** Nearly four decades of reputation. Incredible selection of wagyu and sumptuous ingredients. Superb interiors with smokeless BBQ grillers.
- **Link:** https://www.jojoen.co.jp/en/
- **Phone:** 03-3478-8989

### Ushigoro
- **Area:** Nishiazabu (~8 min drive from Ritz)
- **Hours:** Dinner only
- **Closed:** Never
- **Budget:** ~JPY 10,000
- **Notes:** Only uses A5 rank beef (highest quality Japanese black beef). A la carte and course menus from JPY 6,800+ per person.
- **Link:** http://www.ushigoro.com/en/
- **Phone:** 03-3406-4129
